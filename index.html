A little bit about me.

I didn’t start in code. I originally worked as a Case Manager for Elderly and disabled people while working on my master's for nutrition and certification for health coaching. While I loved learning about health and helping my clients, I found that I liked the interactions I had with computers more. I slowly became the go-to person for troubleshooting computer issues. So I began my journey in IT.

I studied for my CompTIA A+ certification and landed my first IT job as an IT help desk tech. I’m enjoying the role and added responsibility. I've been exposed to networking, cybersecurity, and development. I then turned to Skillcrush and freeCodeCamp to expand my skillset. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. I'm excited to keep growing and learning.
